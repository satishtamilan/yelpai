### Phase 1: Preparation (30 minutes)

#### 1. Test Your App
- [ ] Ensure app works perfectly on your phone
- [ ] Test in a location with multiple businesses
- [ ] Verify AR markers appear correctly
- [ ] Check all UI interactions work smoothly

#### 2. Choose Your Location
- [ ] Pick a busy urban area (downtown, shopping district)
- [ ] Ensure good GPS signal
- [ ] Check for good lighting (daytime, avoid direct sun)
- [ ] Scout for interesting businesses nearby

#### 3. Write Your Script
- [ ] Use the script in `demo-video-script.js` as a template
- [ ] Personalize it with your story
- [ ] Practice reading it out loud
- [ ] Time it (should be ~2:30 minutes)

### Phase 2: Recording (1-2 hours)

#### 1. Screen Recordings

**iOS:**
```
Settings > Control Center > Add Screen Recording
Then: Swipe down > Tap record button > Wait 3 seconds > Start recording
```

**Android:**
```
Swipe down > Look for "Screen Record"
Or: Download "AZ Screen Recorder" from Play Store
```

**What to Record:**
1. Opening the app (loading screen)
2. Permission prompts (camera, location, orientation)
3. AR view with markers appearing
4. Pointing camera in different directions
5. Tapping on AR markers
6. Business details expanding
7. Swiping up the bottom panel
8. Scrolling through business list
9. Tapping refresh button
10. Showing AI tips clearly

**Pro Tips:**
- Record in landscape if possible
- Hold phone steady
- Move camera slowly for AR shots
- Record each segment 2-3 times
- Keep each clip short (10-20 seconds)

#### 2. B-Roll Footage (Optional but Impressive)

- Walking down a street (shows real-world context)
- Pointing phone at buildings
- Close-up of your face looking at phone (shows real person)
- Shots of the actual businesses you're discovering
- Wide shots of the neighborhood

#### 3. Voiceover Recording

**Equipment:**
- Use earbuds with mic or phone's built-in mic
- Record in a quiet room
- Use Voice Memos (iOS) or Voice Recorder (Android)

**Recording Tips:**
- Stand 6-8 inches from mic
- Speak clearly and enthusiastically
- Smile while talking (it changes your voice!)
- Record in segments (easier to edit)
- Record room tone (30 seconds of silence)

### Phase 3: Editing (2-3 hours)

#### Recommended Free Software:

**Mobile:**
- **iMovie** (iOS) - Best for beginners
- **CapCut** (iOS/Android) - Popular, easy to use
- **InShot** (iOS/Android) - Great for quick edits

**Desktop:**
- **DaVinci Resolve** (Free, professional)
- **Shotcut** (Free, open source)
- **iMovie** (Mac, free)

#### Editing Steps:

1. **Import Your Clips**
   - Screen recordings
   - B-roll footage
   - Voiceover audio

2. **Create Timeline** (3:00 target length)
   ```
   0:00-0:20  Intro + title screen
   0:20-0:40  Problem statement
   0:40-1:20  AR demo (main feature)
   1:20-1:50  AI recommendations showcase
   1:50-2:20  Features montage
   2:20-2:45  Technical highlights
   2:45-3:00  Closing + CTA
   ```

3. **Add Music**
   - Download from [YouTube Audio Library](https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary)
   - Pick upbeat, energetic track
   - Lower volume to -20dB when voiceover plays
   - Full volume for intro/outro

4. **Add Text Overlays**
   - App name/logo
   - Feature callouts ("GPS-Based AR", "AI Recommendations")
   - Technical specs
   - Your name and links

5. **Add Transitions**
   - Use simple fades/cuts
   - Don't overdo fancy transitions
   - Match to music beats

6. **Color Grade** (optional)
   - Increase brightness slightly
   - Boost saturation a bit
   - Keep it natural

#### Quality Checklist:
- [ ] Video: 1080p (1920x1080) minimum
- [ ] Audio: Clear voiceover, balanced music
- [ ] Length: 2:45 - 3:00 minutes
- [ ] No dead air or awkward pauses
- [ ] Smooth transitions
- [ ] Text is readable
- [ ] Brand colors consistent

### Phase 4: Export & Upload

**Recommended:**
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Resolution: 1080p (1920x1080)
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Bitrate: 10-15 Mbps
- Audio: AAC, 128kbps
